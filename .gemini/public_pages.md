# Prompt for Public Pages and Their Features

Create all **public-facing pages** for a digital agency website that provides services such as **UI/UX Design**, **Marketing**, **Web Development**, **App Development**, and **Business Consultation**. Each page must be visually polished, consistent, and structured with reusable components.

Below is the complete instruction set for every public page and its required sections.

---

## **1. Home Page — Requirements**
Build a modern, high-impact home page with the following sections:

- **Hero Section**
  - Bold headline
  - Supporting description text
  - Prominent CTA buttons (primary + secondary)
  - Background visual or illustration

- **Services Overview Section**
  - Grid of core services
  - Icons or illustrations
  - Short descriptions

- **Case Study Highlights**
  - Featured successful client projects
  - Metrics or brief results
  - Links to full case studies

- **Testimonials Section**
  - Realistic placeholder client feedback
  - Client name, role, company

- **Agency Highlights / Stats**
  - Years of experience
  - Completed projects
  - Global clients
  - Team size

- **Call to Action Section**
  - Contact or project-starting prompt
  - Strong visual emphasis

---

## **2. About Page — Requirements**
Include the following sections:

- **Introduction Section**
  - Short story about the agency
  - Mission statement

- **Vision & Values**
  - List of values
  - Clear statements about agency principles

- **Team Overview**
  - Profiles of team members
  - Photos, roles, bio highlight

- **Why Choose Us Section**
  - Differentiators
  - Quality assurances (phrased carefully without guaranteeing outcomes)

---

## **3. Services Overview Page — Requirements**
- Grid/list of all services
- Each service includes:
  - Icon or visual
  - Title
  - Short description
  - Learn more link

---

## **4. Individual Service Pages — Requirements**
Create separate detailed pages for each service.

Each page must include:

### **Hero Section**
- Service name
- Summary of the service offering
- Supporting graphic

### **Detailed Description**
- Two to three paragraphs describing the service

### **Feature List**
- Bullet points of features

### **Process Section**
- Step-by-step breakdown (example: Discovery → Design → Development → Launch)

### **Benefits Section**
- Clear statements about how the service supports clients

### **Tools & Technologies**
- Icons or list of tools used

### **CTA Section**
- Contact or project inquiry prompt

---

## **5. Blog List Page — Requirements**
- List of blog posts
- Search or filter options
- Category tags
- Pagination or infinite scroll

---

## **6. Blog Article / Case Study Page — Requirements**
Each article should include:
- Title
- Cover image
- Author information
- Published date
- Full content section
- Case study metrics (for case studies)
- Related articles section

---

## **7. FAQ Page — Requirements**
- Collapsible FAQ items
- Grouping by topics
- Search bar for FAQ items

---

## **8. Contact Page — Requirements**
Include:
- Contact form (name, email, message, phone optional)
- Agency address
- Map placeholder
- Social links

---

## **9. Privacy Policy Page — Requirements**
- Structured legal content sections with placeholder text

---

## **10. Terms & Conditions Page — Requirements**
- Structured terms sections with placeholder text

---

This prompt defines all required public pages and their sections to ensure consistency, maintainability, and a premium user experience.
